IRiS Graduate Research Fellowship
Institute for Research in Sensing (IRiS) at University of Cincinnati
Aug 2023

The IRiS Graduate Research Fellowship was awarded for a project focused on advancing collaborative sensing research through a unique interdisciplinary approach. This fellowship emphasized the integration of STEM and non-STEM disciplines, encouraging the development of innovative solutions to challenges related to sensing and perception. The project involved deep engagement with the creative process, fostering unconventional research collaborations that transcended traditional disciplinary boundaries. By focusing on the sensing lifecycle, the fellowship produced socially and ethically engaged researchers equipped with core competencies in teamwork, communication, and problem-solving, ultimately leading to transformative advancements in the field.
